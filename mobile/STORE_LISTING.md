## Full Description

Transform your fitness journey into a game. Arctivate tracks your workouts, food, and habits — then rewards you with points for every step forward.

KEY FEATURES

- Workout Logging: Log sets, reps, and weight for any exercise. Automatic personal best detection with bonus points.
- AI Coach: Get instant answers about training, nutrition, and recovery from your personal AI fitness coach.
- Food Tracking: Scan meals with your camera for instant calorie and macro estimates, or log manually.
- Habit Streaks: Build daily habits and maintain streaks. The longer you go, the more points you earn.
- Social Feed: Share workouts with friends, give high-fives, and stay motivated together.
- QR Check-ins: Scan QR codes at partner gyms and locations to earn bonus points.
- Group Challenges: Join or create groups, compete on leaderboards, and chat with your crew.
- Points System: Every workout, meal logged, and habit completed earns points. Track your progress on the leaderboard.

Your data stays yours. Arctivate uses end-to-end encryption for authentication and never sells your data.

## App Store Review Notes

This app requires a valid email address to sign in via magic link (passwordless authentication). No test account is needed — the reviewer can use any email address to receive a sign-in link.

The camera is used for:
1. Scanning food items to estimate calories and macros
2. Scanning QR codes at partner gym locations for check-ins

The AI Coach feature connects to our backend API which uses Google Gemini for generating fitness advice responses.
