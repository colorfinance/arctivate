01The Logo
Primary Lockup

Bolt & wordmark.

The Arctivate identity pairs an italic, gradient-filled wordmark with a forward-leaning lightning bolt. The bolt mirrors the italic slant of the type — together they read as motion, energy, and activation.

Variants

Use the horizontal lockup as the default. Switch to stacked, wordmark, or mark when space, scale, or context demand it.

HorizontalDefault for nav, headers, presentations, and signage. Most legible at small scale.

WordmarkWhen the mark is shown nearby or context makes the bolt redundant.

MarkAvatars, favicons, app icons, and any tight square crop.
02Clearspace
Breathing room

Give the bolt room to strike.

Keep a minimum clearspace equal to the width of the bolt around the entire logo. Nothing — type, imagery, edges of the page — should enter that zone.

XX XX

The X rule

The minimum clearspace, X, equals the width of the bolt in the lockup. Multiply it for tighter brand expressions — keep it minimum for cramped UI.

05Color
Palette

Teal across three steps.

The gradient runs 120° from #5EE7D6 → #15B8AE at 55% → #0E8F88. Always preserve all three stops. The ink is reserved for backgrounds and the wordmark in single-color treatments.

On dark

Use ink (#070E14) as the canonical background. The gradient pops against deep navy and reads as motion at any scale.

On light

The gradient holds up on warm off-white. Avoid pure paper-white (#fff) — it cools the brand. Use the cream #f6f4ef instead.

06Typography

Montserrat, end to end.
